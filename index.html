About MediCare Lanka

Leading healthcare provider in Sri Lanka since 2010

Our History

Founded in 2010, MediCare Lanka began as a small clinic with 5 doctors. Today, we are Sri Lanka's largest private hospital network with 25+ branches, 150+ specialist doctors, and over 500,000 patients treated annually. Our journey has been driven by a singular mission: to make world-class healthcare accessible to every Sri Lankan.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, affordable, and world-class healthcare services to all communities in Sri Lanka through innovation, expertise, and patient-centered care.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare institution in South Asia, recognized for clinical excellence, medical education, and research.
